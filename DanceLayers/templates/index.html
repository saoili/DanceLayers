Hello and Welcome to Dance Layers!


Inspired by Julie Brown's excellent solo class at The Spoonful 2016. The idea here is to explore combinations of movement that you might otherwise not do.
Play some blues, get used to each 'layer' and then add another.
They're randomly generated from a predefined list, so some of the combinations may be impossible. Just hit refresh to get a new one. Enjoy!
